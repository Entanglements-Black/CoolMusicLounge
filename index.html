Top 5 Music Genres of the Last Decade

A look at the sounds that defined an era.

1. Hip-Hop/Rap

Dominating the charts and cultural landscape, hip-hop evolved with subgenres like Trap and SoundCloud rap. Artists like Drake, Kendrick Lamar, and Cardi B became global superstars.

2. Pop

Pop music continued its reign with infectious melodies and high-production value. Stars like Taylor Swift, Ariana Grande, and Ed Sheeran defined the sound of mainstream radio.

3. R&B

Contemporary R&B saw a resurgence, blending traditional sounds with electronic and alternative influences. Artists like The Weeknd, Frank Ocean, and SZA pushed the genre's boundaries.

4. Latin

Latin music exploded globally, with Reggaeton and Latin Trap crossing over into mainstream markets. "Despacito" became a worldwide phenomenon, and artists like Bad Bunny and J Balvin topped charts everywhere.

5. Electronic/Dance

EDM continued to be a festival staple, while subgenres like Lo-fi hip hop and chillwave gained massive popularity on streaming platforms as the go-to music for focus and relaxation.
